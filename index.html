Partnership with HD Hyundai

Anduril has partnered with HD Hyundai Heavy Industries to develop these cutting-edge autonomous surface vessels. The first prototype will be fabricated in South Korea, with subsequent vessels built in the United States as part of the Navy's Modular Attack Surface Craft (MASC) competition.
